Speaking Club

Join our club to drastically improve your English, learn about different university majors, and gain friends from all over the world.
Last month, we were honoured to receive 90 registrations for only 30 membership spots in our international speaking club.
We admitted a group of impressively accomplished students representing Yale, Williams, NYUAD, and UChicago, to name a few.
Each session will last for 1.5 hours with a short lecture, two breakout room discussions, and a Q&A session. Our international speakers and facilitators, representing the world's best universities, will also send you readings and topic vocabulary in advance.

Price: 24.99 per month (19.99 for Freshman Alumni)

Admissions Program

Freshman's brand-new Admissions Program is a premium 3-month course that will lay the foundation for your future success in the admissions process.

Freshman's international team hails from Austria, Brazil, Canada, Kazakhstan, Russia, Singapore, USA, and Uzbekistan. Our worldwide network of admissions mentors will support you in your university applications.
Starting 15th May, this three-month program will give you full access to:
  • Exclusive Admissions and Writing Workshops led by Higher Education Professionals
  • Academic Advising and Personalised Feedback Sessions for your Writing and Reflections
  • World-class International Opportunities and Diverse Extra-Curricular Activities
  • Full access to Freshman's Advanced English, SAT English, and SAT Math

Total Price: $999

Essay Mentorship

Over the past two years, we have helped only seven students with their college essays. 100% of them were accepted to high-quality universities, ranging from NYU Abu Dhabi to Yale University.
Given that none of our students came from fancy private schools, won international olympiads, or achieved anything of that effect, we had to rely primarily on our ability to craft exciting narratives that would best position our students in the competitive application process.
This year, we offer the College Essay Mentorship whereby we will hold one-on-one consultations with students to help them brainstorm their strongest storylines, write up drafts, and edit their final narratives.
