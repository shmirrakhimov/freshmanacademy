Who We Are

Freshman Academy was founded in 2019 by a team of Yale-NUS students including Valera Arakelyan, who had studied in a typical public school in Uzbekistan. Over these 3 years we have evolved from a test preparatory agency to an international team of successful students and education experts who help applicants to gain admission to top universities around the world. Our goal is to give everyone equal chances

We have prepared 100+ students

Our graduates study at the world's best universities such as Yale, UChicago, UPenn and Brown
