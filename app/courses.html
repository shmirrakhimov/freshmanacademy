Advanced English

Advanced English is designed to help you get your level of English to an entirely new level.
As a part of the class, you will be introduced to texts from a range of disciplines: including philosophy, literature, science, and more. You will learn to critically assess these texts and effectively communicate your thoughts in your written works. This class is perfect for those who want to get a taste of and prepare for quality international education. Note that Advanced English will most certainly be the most challenging English class you have ever had. However, our teaching experience shows that surviving the pressure of this program will indicate your readiness in applying to the world's top universities.
